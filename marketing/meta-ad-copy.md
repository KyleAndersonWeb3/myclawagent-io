## Notes for Testing

- Run variants 1 and 3 against each other first — pain-point vs. price tends to be the most revealing split
- Variant 2 (curiosity) typically performs best on cold traffic but has lower conversion rate — optimize for link clicks, not purchases
- Variant 5 (direct offer) works best after someone has already seen 2+ ads — don't lead with it on cold audiences
- All hooks are under 125 characters for primary text compliance
- Test with static image creative (dark background, orange accent, minimal text) to match brand

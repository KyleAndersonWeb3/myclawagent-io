These are jumping-off points. You know your audience. Riff, remix, or go completely original.

**Hard no:** Anything that makes AI feel scary, robotic, or like it's replacing people's jobs in a negative way.

### 1 — The Storytime
Tell a story (real or hypothetical) about being buried in work. Emails piling up. Missed follow-ups. Forgetting a meeting. Then the pivot: "I got a MyClawAgent and now my agent sends me a morning briefing before I've had coffee. I haven't opened my inbox on my own in two weeks."

### 2 — The Discovery React
Screen record yourself finding myclawagent.io for the first time. React genuinely. "Wait — they set this up FOR you? Same day? And it just... texts you on WhatsApp? And handles your email? Hold on, let me read this again."

### 3 — The List
"Things my AI agent handled while I was asleep last night:" — rapid-fire: flagged 3 urgent emails, drafted 2 replies, prepped my 9am meeting, followed up with a lead I forgot about, updated the CRM. Pause. "I used to do all of that myself."

### 4 — The POV
POV: You just booked a kickoff call with MyClawAgent at 2pm. By 6pm your agent is live, connected to your email and calendar, texting you on Telegram. Show the moment the first briefing comes in. That "oh this is real" face.

### 5 — The Glow-Up
Before: Checking email at midnight, missing follow-ups, spending Sunday catching up. After: Your agent handled it. Show the contrast — stressed, reactive, drowning vs. calm, informed, ahead of everything.

### 6 — The Skit
Two founders. One is grinding through their inbox manually at 11pm. The other is at dinner because their agent handled it. One message to the phone, agent confirms everything's covered. "I have a MyClawAgent. What's your excuse?"

### 7 — The Hot Take
Straight to camera: "Hiring a full-time EA costs $60,000 a year. A MyClawAgent costs $3,000 to set up and $200 a month. It works 24/7. It doesn't call in sick. And it's live the same day you book a call. I'm not saying fire your EA. I'm saying — why are you still doing this yourself?"

### 8 — The Trend Jack
Use a trending sound or format and make it about finally getting leverage in your business. MyClawAgent is the payoff or punchline.

### 9 — The Walkthrough
Green screen myclawagent.io behind you. Walk through it while reacting. "You book a call. They set everything up same day. Your agent connects to your Gmail, your calendar, your CRM. And then it just... works? Forever? For $200 a month? This is unhinged and I love it."

### 10 — The Confession
"I used to be the founder who answered every email personally. Handled every follow-up. Checked Slack at midnight. I thought that was just the job. Then I got a MyClawAgent. I haven't opened my inbox unprompted in three weeks. I'm a different person. A calmer person. A more profitable person."

## Do's & Don'ts

| ✅ DO | ❌ DON'T |
|---|---|
| Be yourself — your personality is why we picked you | Read a script word for word |
| Hook in the first 2 seconds — scroll-stopper is everything | Make it sound like a corporate tech ad |
| Make it feel like telling a friend, not reading an ad | Promise specific outcomes ("your business will 10x") |
| Say "MyClawAgent" by name at least once | Call it a chatbot or compare it to ChatGPT |
| Show or mention myclawagent.io | Use copyrighted music — use trending Reels/TikTok audio |
| Use humor, ambition, relatability — whatever fits your voice | Bury the brand — MyClawAgent should be memorable |
| Show the founder lifestyle angle — time, freedom, leverage | Make AI feel scary or dystopian |

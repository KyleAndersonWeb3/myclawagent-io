Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you book your call.

What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open-source AI assistant that runs 24/7 on your own infrastructure. Unlike ChatGPT or Claude, it works proactively — scanning your inbox, managing your calendar, updating your CRM — without you having to ask.

Do I need to be technical to use your service?

Not at all. That's the whole point. We handle the install, security hardening, and every integration. You just tell us what you want automated and we make it happen.

How long does setup take?

Most agents go live the same day as your kickoff call. We deploy, test, and hand you a working agent — usually within a few hours.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your agent runs on your own machine or private VPS — never on shared infrastructure. Every deployment follows the official OpenClaw security hardening guide, including firewall rules and secure credential storage.

What messaging apps does the agent work with?

Telegram, Discord, or WhatsApp — your choice. Most people pick the one they're already using for work.

What happens after the 14-day hypercare period?

You can move to a monthly retainer for ongoing maintenance, workflow reviews, and priority support. Or take it from there yourself — the agent we built is fully yours either way.

What's the difference between Starter and Full Agent?

Starter covers the core setup — email, calendar, one messaging platform, security hardening, and 14-day support. Full Agent adds CRM, Slack, and custom integrations, plus ongoing monthly maintenance so your agent keeps improving over time.

Can you set up agents for multiple people on my team?

Yes. Each team member gets their own dedicated agent with access to exactly what they need. Many teams deploy 2–6 agents — a CEO agent, a sales agent, a finance agent — all coordinating through shared context.
